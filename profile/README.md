## 💡 Our Vision

In the realm of embedded systems and smart infrastructure, the most inescapable cages are not vendor encryption algorithms—they are the dogmatic "checklists" and "tool-dependencies" that confine a researcher’s mind.

**OOB IoT SEC Labs** refuses to be a textbook assembly line. We forge **creative, independent security researchers who break constraints and reconstruct technical intuition.** While our primary battlefield is the vast ecosystem of IoT & Connected Devices, our methodology natively integrates Advanced Binary Analysis, Artificial Intelligence, and Security Development to dismantle systems from first principles.

---

## 🏗️ Research & Training Modules

We approach security not as an isolated discipline, but as a convergence of critical technical domains:

### ⚡ IoT & Hardware Security (Core)
*   **Physical Layer Breach:** Moving past standard debugging interfaces (JTAG/UART).
*   **Hardware Attacks:** Side-channel analysis and advanced fault injection (Glitch).
*   **Trust Chain Disruption:** Bypassing secure boot chains in firmware and physical silicon.

### 🎯 Advanced Binary Analysis
*   **Vulnerability Mechanics:** Deep-diving into software vulnerability roots.
*   **Automated Discovery:** Leveraging static analysis and dynamic fuzzer development.
*   **Deep-Level 0-days:** Unearthing high-impact zero-days in complex kernels and user-space binaries.

### 🤖 AI-Powered Research
*   **Automation at Scale:** Weaponizing AI and symbolic execution to automate the discovery of elusive code paths.
*   **Logic Flaw Hunting:** Scaling offensive capabilities beyond human analytical limitations.

### 🛠️ Security Development
*   **Tool Makers:** Shifting from tool users to tool makers.
*   **Custom Infrastructure:** Building custom fuzzers, firmware analysis pipelines, and reliable exploit orchestration scripts from absolute zero.
